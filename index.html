He lives by the motto that:"If you do what you love for a job, you'll never work a day in your life".

Some of his past jobs include:

  • Trained extra on a blockbuster film set

Chris discovered a passion for coding while working 3 years in customer support of a leading gaming brand.
He started to further develop his understanding of code by learning how to develop a game in Python using Pygame.
However, this only further fuelled his passion for coding and soon he also sign up can completed short courses in Kotlin, Swift, Ruby, C# and even SQL.
